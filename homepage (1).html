Who are Plannerz?

We are Event organizers who can plan anything that you want from a Prom event to Huge Musical parties. Since 1990 Plannerz has been helping clients solve problems. We provide audio visual equipment and event production services for meetings, galas, fundraisers, rallies, concerts, races, commencements, and other gatherings. Work with our team to create an experience that matches your vision.
